Delete your account

You can delete your CoLift account and data at any time, right from the app.

In the app

  • Open CoLift and go to the You tab.
  • Tap the Settings gear (top right).
  • Scroll to Delete account.
  • Type DELETE to confirm, then tap Delete forever.

What gets removed

Deleting your account permanently erases your profile, streak, workout history, moments, and any CoLift+ logs (food, weight, goals). This cannot be undone.

CoLift+ subscribers

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel a paid subscription. Cancel CoLift+ first in iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions to stop future charges.

Need help?

If you can't access the app, email colift.app.official@gmail.com from your account email and we'll delete your account and data for you.
