Community Guidelines

Last updated: June 2026

CoLift is about showing up for a friend — not flexing, not performing. These rules keep it that way. By using CoLift you agree to follow them. Breaking them can get content removed or your account suspended.

Be supportive

Encourage, don't tear down. Reactions and comments should lift people up. We automatically filter abusive language, and we'll act on anything that slips through.

Not allowed

  • Harassment, bullying, threats, or hate based on who someone is.
  • Nudity, sexual content, or graphic violence.
  • Impersonation or pretending to be someone you're not.
  • Spam, scams, or promoting dangerous behavior (including disordered-eating or extreme-cut content).
  • Anything illegal.

Keep it real, keep it kind

Share your own workouts and moments. Don't post other people without their okay. No body-shaming — yours or anyone's. CoLift has no leaderboards on purpose; the win is showing up.

Report & block

See something off? Tap the menu on any post to report it, or block a user. We review reports and remove violating content. There's no tolerance for objectionable content or abusive users.

Questions

Reach us anytime at colift.app.official@gmail.com.
