Questions

What is CoLift?

A social fitness app where you train with a friend, like BeReal, but for the gym. You do your workout, they do theirs, and every session becomes a shared post you both react to.

What are "both your reactions"?

When a workout posts, you each drop a reaction selfie: a real photo of you, not just an emoji (think BeReal's RealMoji). Both reactions sit on the post together, so it's a shared moment, not a like count.

Do we have to work out at the same time or place?

No. Catch a friend's lift live, or replay their exact workout later, at different gyms and different times. The post shows both clocks, so 6am and 7pm still land as one session.

Is there any competition or ranking?

No. CoLift rewards showing up, not your weights or PRs. No leaderboards, no ranking. The win is the habit, together. We call it presence over performance.

Is it free?

Yes. The core (presence, invites, the feed, replays, reactions, streaks) is free forever. CoLift+ is an optional upgrade (~$60/yr) that adds tracking, analytics, and food & weight logging, with a free 14-day trial and no card to start.

Is my data private?

You control who sees your workouts. We don't sell your data or run ads. See our Privacy Policy.
