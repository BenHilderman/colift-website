Questions

What is CoLift?

A social fitness app where you train with a friend — like BeReal, but for the gym. You do your workout, they do theirs, and you show up for each other.

Do we have to work out at the same time or place?

No. Catch a friend's lift live, or replay their exact workout later — different gyms, different times. You train together without being in the same room.

Is it free?

Yes. The core — presence, invites, the feed, replays, streaks — is free forever. CoLift+ is an optional upgrade (~$60/yr) that adds tracking, analytics, and food & weight logging, with a free 14-day trial and no card to start.

What does "presence over performance" mean?

CoLift rewards showing up, not your weights or PRs. No leaderboards, no ranking. The win is the habit, together.

Is my data private?

You control who sees your workouts. We don't sell your data or run ads. See our Privacy Policy.
