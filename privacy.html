Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
DRAFT — have a lawyer review before App Store submission

CoLift ("we," "us") makes a social fitness app where you train alongside a friend. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the control you have. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not run ads.

What we collect

  • Account info — your name and the sign-in identifier you use (e.g. Apple/Google or phone).
  • Your activity — workouts you log, moments you capture, streaks, reactions, and comments.
  • Optional content — photos/video you choose to capture during a workout.
  • CoLift+ logs (optional) — food, calories, macros, body weight, and goals you enter.
  • Device & usage — basic diagnostics to keep the app working (crash and performance data).

How we use it

To run the core experience (showing your presence to friends you've added, your feed, streaks, and tracking), to keep the app reliable and safe, and to contact you about your account. That's it.

Who can see your activity

You control who sees your workouts and moments. By default they're shared only with friends you've connected with. We do not make your content public without your action.

Sharing

We use a small number of service providers (hosting, crash reporting, payment processing through Apple) strictly to operate the app. We do not sell your data and we do not share it for advertising.

Your choices & rights

  • Edit or remove content you've posted at any time.
  • Delete your account and associated data in-app — see Delete account.
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing us.

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we're required to retain limited records by law.

Children

CoLift is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). We don't knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We'll post any changes here and update the date above.

Contact
