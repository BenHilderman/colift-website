Common questions

How do I add a friend?

Open CoLift, tap to invite, and share your link. Once they accept, you'll see each other's presence and feed.

How do I manage or cancel CoLift+?

CoLift+ is billed through your Apple Account. Manage or cancel anytime in iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions. Cancelling stops future charges; your current period stays active until it ends.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and data directly in the app. Step-by-step: Delete account.

Is my data private?

Yes — you control who sees your workouts, and we never sell your data or run ads. See the Privacy Policy.
